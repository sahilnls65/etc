Privacy Policy – Emgage Work Hour Calculator

The Emgage Work Hour Calculator extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All calculations are performed locally in the user's browser. The extension only reads information displayed on the Emgage timesheet website (such as Net Working Hours and Punch In/Out records) in order to calculate the estimated workday finish time.

No data is sent to any external servers, analytics services, or third parties.

The extension does not track browsing activity outside of the Emgage timesheet website.

If you have questions regarding this extension, please contact the developer.
